3D Graphic

 

The Graphic3d package allows creating 3d graphic objects in a viewer.  These objects, called structures, are made up of groups of primitives and attributes.   A group is the smallest editable element of a structure.   A structure can be displayed, erased, and highlighted.   A transformation can be applied to it.   Structures can be connected to form a hierarchy of structures, composed by transformations.   The viewer can perform global manipulation of structures.

The Visual3d package contains the group of classes required to implement commands for a 3D viewer. The viewer manages views and light sources.
